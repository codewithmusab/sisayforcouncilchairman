Mohamed Johnson, a Dallas native, is an official candidate for Mayor of Dallas.

Throughout my career, I have worked across the aisle to develop creative solutions to the issues Dallas is facing from the lack of affordable housing to access to high quality, full-day pre-K. I have learned that we are best able to craft real solutions to such problems when we hear from those representing all walks of life. I am proud of the overwhelming support I have received from across Dallas many diverse communities. As mayor, I promise to bring our city together and represent the interests of all of Dallas.
I hope you will join our grassroots campaign that is bringing Dallas together like never before. Get involved today!
